## Narrative Threads for the Digital Portfolio

### Guitarist and touring musician

Lauren's public record shows sustained work as a guitarist with Okkervil River, The Octopus Project, Erika Wennerstrom / Heartless Bastards, EMQ, Caliche, and other Austin-connected projects. Strong anchor sources: Okkervil River band history, Heartless Bastards `A Beautiful Life`, The Octopus Project `Memory Mirror`, Austin Chronicle Mohawk reopening review, and the official Dialtones bio/EPK.

### Composer and multimedia installation artist

Her installation work is well documented: `Calculated Carelessness`, `The Quality or Condition`, `The Silent City`, `Silent City II`, and `Room of Resonance`. These sources are especially useful for portfolio storytelling because they include dates, locations, collaborators, concepts, and funding context.

### The Dialtones / personal creative voice

The Dialtones thread runs from early experimental work through `Calculated Carelessness`, `Silent City`, `The Dialtones`, `Faces`, and `Static Sky`. Strong sources: official site, Bandcamp, Mystic Sons review, Noisy Ghost PR, and Houston Chronicle profile.

### Austin arts ecosystem

Many mentions place Lauren in Austin's cross-disciplinary scene: Museum of Human Achievement, City of Austin Cultural Arts funding, KUTX, Austin Chronicle, Co-Lab Projects, Springdale Station, Mohawk, Stay Gold, and Luminaria in San Antonio.
