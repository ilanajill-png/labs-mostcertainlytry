Four Throughlines

The sources point to a career shaped by collaboration and reinvention: touring guitar work, authored experimental projects, Austin arts infrastructure, and The Dialtones as a personal creative home.

Guitarist and Touring Musician

Okkervil River, The Octopus Project, Heartless Bastards, EMQ, Caliche, and related Austin projects form the performance backbone of the archive.

Composer and Installation Artist

Calculated Carelessness, The Quality or Condition, The Silent City, Silent City II, and Room of Resonance show recurring interest in interactive, immersive work.

The Dialtones

A long-running vehicle for experimental rock, intermedia composition, and later releases including Faces and Static Sky.

Austin Arts Ecosystem

Museum of Human Achievement, KUTX, Austin Chronicle, City of Austin cultural funding, Co-Lab Projects, and Mohawk recur across the record.
